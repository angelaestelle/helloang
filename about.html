Originally an illustrator growing up playing lots of video games, I've always felt strong connections with colours, stories, and the reasons behind them. My background began in graphic design and illustration – Copic markers, pigma pens – however I fell in love with the digital space when I began to post-process drawings through Photoshop. Through natural progression, I moved into flyer gigs, clothing production, marketing collateral, then designing ads and web experiences.

After working at an ad agency and studying Computer Science, I felt empowered once I was able to start connecting all the moving parts into functional, accessible design. Being able to compartmentalize beforehand how something is built gave me extra confidence with my design decisions.

AFK I absolutely adore my cat, Stella, drinking coffee, gin & tonics, and am regularly hunting for new music. I love to get my nails done and discover new beauty products and hacks. Between all these things you can catch me watching Bob's Burgers or playing video games.
