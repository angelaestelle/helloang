MoneyBag

Product, UI, UX

Problem:

We identified a challenge amongst a large demographic of consumers in this digital age. A large number including Millennials (22-37), and some falling within the Boomer category (38-45) and some in early Gen Z (19-21) often have difficulty keeping track of their big and little expenses, and find themselves wanting to regain full control of their spending, including the popular subscription business model. Upon several rounds of testing and surveying, we’ve been able to validate the idea of creating an app that records your purchases and notifies you of refund/expiry dates and subscription charges.

App Goals:

  • Track available trial periods, warranties, and exchange policies for user purchases
  • Consolidate terms and conditions for returning multiple types of products and services
  • Alert users of trial and exchange periods close to expiry 
  • Assist users with the process to cancel or return products and services
