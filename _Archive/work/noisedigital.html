Noise Digital

Visual design, UI

In 2017, Noise Digital launched a rebrand and site refresh. While working with the art director and front-end developer I focused on designing UI, standardizing, and building the case studies page and template on the Noise website.

I initially focused on doing a competitive analysis with other top tier agencies’ case studies, and integrating certain elements with the experience provided for any existing pages designed. I worked closely with the developer to make sure the functionality for both the front-end and the back (CMS) checked every box.
