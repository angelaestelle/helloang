TinMates

Product, UI, UX

Problem:

Everyday, there are thousands of individuals that are sent to prisons and correction facilities that deprive them of a basic human need: human connection. There is proven study that the more isolated individuals become, the more dangerous their lives can get – they can harm themselves or turn to harming others. In turn, this makes it more difficult to rehabilitate. Knowing there are many barriers that make this experience difficult, one of the first steps we can take to removing the stigma and lifting these barriers, is to create a bridge.

App Goals:

TinMates allows everyday people to connect with incarcerated individuals through a mobile app by sending letters, gifts, or scheduling visits. Users can browse through profiles and learn about inmates before contacting them.
