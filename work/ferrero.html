Ferrero Brand Social

Visual design, UI, video, social campaigns

Under the Ferrero brand, I worked with Pralines, Nutella, and TicTac on various projects across their social media and websites. I contributed to their art direction, asset design, social media content creation, and website design for their campaigns and events. Below is the Nutella Breakfast Challenge for their Spife 3.0 (yup, spoon & knife) campaign. We wanted to drive customers to purchase a Nutella jar marked with a Spife sticker, which gave them the chance to win one. For the challenge, we had celebrity chef Stefano Faita join us to show three different ways people could use the Spife for breakfast meals. In return of generating awareness of the promotion, users shared them winning and their own content through Facebook & Instagram using #NutellaSpife. Nutella wanted these videos to be reflected on a microsite experience, so I got to design the Breakfast Challenge assets and the landing page. The videos are also accompanied by other recipe recommendations that drive you to the Nutella website.

In 2017 Nutella ran another summer tour across Canada, “The Nutella Tour.” They went from city-to-city, via food truck and Fiat, delivering Nutella meals and Nutella-to-Go snacks to their customers. We ran a 2-month social campaign to raise awareness and drive customers to the event. Through Facebook and Instagram, I designed all the social content provided through this campaign.

I designed all the social content for Facebook and Instagram for Ferrero Rocher. I got to select photo assets and design assets specifically for their campaigns.

I designed all the social content for Facebook and Instagram for Ferrero Rocher and TicTac. I got to select photo assets, design assets specifically for their campaigns, and produce video content. We also worked with Disney to promote movie collaborations with TicTac.
