BestBuy.ca

Digital Experience

At BestBuy Canada I had the opportunity to help with designing weekly sales and product launches – this includes phone launches for the iPhone XS Max & XR, iPad Pro, Microsoft Surface 2, and all major holiday sales such as Black Friday, Cyber Monday, Boxing Day, and January’s Clear Out Sale. We worked closely with vendors and our marketing team to align on strategy and design.

With UX, copy, and other major stakeholders, we worked together to improve web experiences with a focus on the customer.

I was able to bring design problems to our Community Practice, and together we engaged in design critiques so we could identify solutions as a team. We worked together using agile methodologies to improve how we work and communicate.
