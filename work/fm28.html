FM28.co

About the project

Designing the website for Fm28.co, which is an artist collective based out of Los Angeles.

The Challenge

The FM28 website was provided with very limited information, so we had to fill up the gaps with intriguing visuals. This is also the portal for all kinds of industry creatives to join the collective.

The Solution

We created plenty animations, imagery, and video that we spread across the site to fill up some of the negative space.

Site Map

The site map was simple - FM28 just needed its core pages to get itself up and running.

Wireframing

I worked around one of the director’s biggest requests for the site: don’t make the change look too drastic. So, I worked around the already existing layout structure and columns so the CCNC team doesn’t feel like they have to relearn how to use their website. For the most part, information was being shuffled.

Final product

Reorganizing the entire site was instrumental to improving its usability. CCNC has so much important information for such a wide audience, this first step was foundational. We removed several sections and shuffled them where they would make more sense for the users. I spent a few days speaking with their team about the priorities and goals for each section which, in turn, resulted in a more cohesive and concise site structure.
You can view it here at CCNCSJ.ca
