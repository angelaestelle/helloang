Work About me Project: ADDB Email Campaigns

About the project

A collection of a few email campaigns I designed and shipped for pharma clients Gilead and Abbott, while I was at Anderson DDB. I leveraged the assets provided by the client.

Gilead - IBD Needs Innovation

Designing the accompanying emails for Gilead’s IBD Needs Innovation website and conference. These marketing emails detail Gileads ongoing progression and research for inflammatory bowel disease.

Gilead - TimeForJAKSTAT

Emails for Gilead’s TimeforJAKSTAT campaign, conference, and website launch which supports their IBD progression and research.

Abbott - eCRM emails

Designing Abbott’s eCRM marketing emails which provide information on their diabetes monitoring products, healthcare and patient workshops, product tips, and conferences.

Abbott - Groupstart Workshop emails

Designing Abbott’s eCRM marketing emails which provide information on their diabetes monitoring products, healthcare and patient workshops, product tips, and conferences.
