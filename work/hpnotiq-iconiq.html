Work About me Project: Iconiq Session

About the project

Designing and building Hpnotiq's latest campaign site Iconiq Session, which is a contest for users to get a chance to share a song with Fat Joe or Jim Jones.

The Challenge

Hpnotiq had 1 week to launch a new campaign called Iconiq Session, which is a competition to get on a song with rappers Fat Joe & Jim Jones. We had 1 week until launch and needed a feasible solution beyond developing a site from scratch. That means leveraging CMS sites and API's.

Wireframing

Iconiq Session is a small microsite requiring 3 things: an age gate, a form, and a space for the legal conditions. We made sure to leave plenty of room for Hpnotiqs brand assets to stand out.
