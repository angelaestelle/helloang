Gilead

Gilead Sciences is a research-based global pharmaceuticals company focused on developing commercialized medicine. JAKSTAT in particular is about Gilead’s developments on rheumatology & gastroenterology.

TeachJAKSTAT originated from LearnJAKSTAT.com – the purpose for Teach is to provide HCPs (healthcare providers) teaching material and content to teach colleagues about JAKSTAT. This includes images, videos, PPT slides with interactive prompts, and case study patient profiles. These sections are separated by Educational Images, Educational Videos, Teaching Material and Course Content. Users can’t access any of these pages unless they’re registered, so unregistered users will only see a login gate.

During sign up, a user has to specify they’re an HCP – only registered HCP’s are allowed to access this information for confidentiality. Any of the content throughout the site is downloadable for their own use.

The challenge for pharma projects involve massive amounts of legal parameters, liabilities, endless amounts of data, context-driven visuals, and safety information that typically isn’t up for discussion in terms of visiblity.

This project is actively being updated and reiterated upon – the images on this page are currently what’s in production.
